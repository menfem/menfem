why need to understand basics of electricity markets, what interests me. These posts will consist of me teaching the basics of electricity markets in order to further improve my own understanding and as a further reference to myself

focus today was on fixed costs.

- capital cost of building a plant and financing a plant; consists of cost of construction (overnight cost), payment to the owners of capital (interest rate) and we can think of the capital cost in terms of the amortised loan amount over term of loan. So in order to pay back the loan we are going to need to pay back the initial capital that we borrowed and the interest payment on that capital. All of that will be put togehter in one payment the amortised loan amount. These fixed costs are fixed over the lifetime of the plant and are reported as a monthly cost and it can also be described as the monthly rental price of capital.

Thus once we know the amount of capital, interest rate and term of the loan we can create a payment function. For example, using a baseload powerplant with 500MW
